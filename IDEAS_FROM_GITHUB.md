## Recommended Features to Implement

### High Priority (Easy to Add)
1. **Daily Rewards System**
   - Users get bonus coins for logging in daily
   - Streak counter (7 days = bonus)
   - Calendar view of rewards

2. **Tap-to-Earn Mini Game**
   - Add a tap button/coin users can tap
   - Each tap earns coins (with cooldown/energy system)
   - Visual feedback and animations

3. **Surprise Boxes/Chests**
   - Random rewards (coins, boosters, etc.)
   - Open after completing X tasks
   - Visual opening animation

4. **Social Media Tasks**
   - Follow Twitter account
   - Join Telegram channel
   - Retweet post
   - Each task gives coins

### Medium Priority (More Complex)
5. **Booster System**
   - 2x coins for 1 hour
   - Auto-tap booster
   - Energy refill
   - Purchase with coins

6. **Multi-Level Referral**
   - Level 1: Direct referrals (5 coins)
   - Level 2: Referral's referrals (2 coins)
   - Level 3: 3rd level (1 coin)

7. **Achievement System**
   - Badges for milestones
   - "Complete 10 tasks" badge
   - "Earn 1000 coins" badge
   - "Refer 5 people" badge

8. **Energy System**
   - Limited taps per day
   - Energy refills over time
   - Can buy energy with coins

### Low Priority (Future)
9. **Wallet Integration**
   - Connect TON wallet
   - Withdraw coins to wallet
   - Crypto payouts

10. **Passive Income**
    - Invest coins in "businesses"
    - Earn passive income over time
    - Different investment tiers

11. **Combo System**
    - Tap in rhythm for combo multiplier
    - Higher combo = more coins per tap

## Key Takeaways

1. **Tap-to-Earn is Core**: Most successful apps have an actual tapping game
2. **Daily Engagement**: Daily rewards keep users coming back
3. **Social Tasks**: Easy way to grow community
4. **Visual Feedback**: Animations and effects make it engaging
5. **Progression Systems**: Levels, achievements, and milestones keep users motivated
